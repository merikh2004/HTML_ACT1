Pancit Malabon
  INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 cup annatto oil (achiote oil)
  • 1 tbsp shrimp paste (bagoong alamang)
  • 1 medium onion, minced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup pork belly, sliced thinly
  • 1 cup shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/2 cup squid, cleaned and sliced into rings
  • 1 cup shrimp broth (made from boiling shrimp shells)
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 3 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup pork rinds (chicharon), crushed
  • 500g thick rice noodles (malabon noodles or bihon)
  • 1 cup Napa cabbage, blanched and chopped
  • 1/2 cup hard-boiled eggs, sliced
  • 1/4 cup green onions, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon toasted garlic bits
  • 1/4 cup tinapa flakes (flaked smoked fish)
  • Calamansi wedges, for serving
 		  PROCEDURE

  1. Prepare the Sauce: Heat annatto oil in a large pot or wok over medium heat. Add shrimp paste and cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
  2. SautÃ© Aromatics: Add onion and garlic, cook until softened, about 2 minutes. Stir in pork belly and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Seafood Time: Add shrimp and squid, cook until pink and opaque, about 2-3 minutes.
  4. Flavorful Broth: Pour in shrimp broth and water, season with fish sauce and black pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Rice Noodles: While the sauce simmers, cook the rice noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  6. Crispy Delight: Add half of the crushed chicharon to the simmering sauce, reserving the rest for topping.
  7. Time to Combine: Remove the pan from heat and gently mix in the cooked noodles.
  8. Serve it Up: Transfer the pancit to a serving platter. Arrange blanched cabbage, sliced eggs, green onions, toasted garlic bits, tinapa flakes, and remaining chicharon on top.
  9. Citrusy Kick: Serve with calamansi wedges on the side, allowing everyone to squeeze fresh citrus for their desired tangy flavor.
Pancit Malabon is a vibrant Filipino dish featuring thick rice noodles in a rich shrimp sauce,
typically packed with various seafood and topped with delicious accompaniments.

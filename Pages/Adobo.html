Chicken Adobo
  INGREDIENTS

  • 1kg (2.2lb) chicken pieces (thighs, legs, breasts, or a mix)
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup white vinegar
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 10 whole black peppercorns
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • Salt to taste


 		  PROCEDURE
  1. Wash and pat dry the chicken pieces.
  2. In a large bowl, combine soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaf, and peppercorns. Add chicken pieces and marinate for at least 30 minutes, or up to overnight for deeper flavor.
  3. Heat oil in a pot or Dutch oven over medium heat.
  4. Sear the chicken pieces until golden brown on all sides.
  5. Pour the marinade into the pot with the chicken. Add 1/2 cup water and bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce heat, cover the pot, and simmer for 30-40 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through and tender.
  7. During the last 10 minutes of simmering, you can adjust the consistency of the sauce:
  8. Season with salt to taste.
  9. Serve hot with rice and enjoy!
Chicken adobo, also known as adobong manok, is a quintessential filipino braised chicken,
marinated and stewed with vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, bay leaves, black peppercorns

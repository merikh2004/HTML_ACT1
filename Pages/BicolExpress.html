Bicol Express
  INGREDIENTS

  • 500g pork belly, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb ginger, minced
  • 2-3 Thai chilies, sliced (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • 1-2 green finger chilies, sliced (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • 2 tablespoons bagoong alamang (shrimp paste)
  • 1 Knorr pork cube (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
 		  PROCEDURE

  1. Heat oil in a pot or wok over medium heat. Add onion, garlic, and ginger, and cook until fragrant.
  2. Add pork belly and cook until browned on all sides.
  3. Pour in coconut milk and water. Add Thai chilies, green finger chilies, and bagoong alamang. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30-45 minutes, or until pork is tender and sauce is thickened.
  5. Add Knorr pork cube (optional) and adjust seasoning if needed.
  6. Serve hot with steamed rice and enjoy!
Bicol Express is a famous stew made with gata (coconut milk) and wild chilies. Traditionally, pork and balaw (shrimp paste) take centerstage,
cooking in a luscious, fiery sauce.
